PAPER BIRDS

One of my friends once told me that there are approximately the same number of people living in Slovenia as in Berlin, so if I only write in Slovene… well, you know what that means. Thanks to my beautiful translators ❤ my stories are now available in English – at least a few of them.
With words I built very different worlds, but I chose to translate only those I like the most, at least for the time being.
Hope you’ll enjoy exploring them.
