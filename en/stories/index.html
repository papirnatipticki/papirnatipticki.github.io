About me

Obviously, I am the author of the short stories published here. I am a psychologist, currently (still) floating through life. I have many interests, but not much patience to dedicate myself to only one of them. Most of the time you’ll find me reading, hiking, dancing, traveling, enjoying the music and socializing (inside the limits of a typical introvert, of course). My name is Tanja. If I had a penny for every time someone said I look familiar after I introduce myself, I’d be rich by now. But guess what? Reading makes you richer as well, so I’m glad you read.
